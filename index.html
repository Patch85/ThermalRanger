Thermal Ranger

Actively monitor wort temperatures during primary and secondary fermentation.

Yeast health during fermentation is directly connected to the temperature of fermenting wort. Being aware of the fermentation temperature is the first step towards controlling it.

Was that last batch just right? Want to make it again? Having a history of that last batch's fermentation temperatures gives you the information you need to create the same environment for another stellar brew.

Future plans for Thermal Ranger include the ability to control your fermentation environment's cooling and heating components, allowing for actively managed fermentation profiles.
